Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is there an Albert Shoes loyalty program?

What materials are used in Albert Shoes' products?

Do Albert Shoes come in half sizes?

How do I clean and care for my Albert Shoes?
* Care instructions vary by product. Please refer to the specific care instructions provided with each pair of shoes.
Do Albert Shoes offer gift cards?
* Yes, we offer gift cards that can be purchased online or in-store.

How often does Albert Shoes release new styles?
* We release new styles every season. Stay tuned to our website and social media channels for updates.
Does Albert Shoes offer discounts for bulk orders?
* Yes, we offer discounts for bulk orders. Please contact our customer service for more information.
What payment methods does Albert Shoes accept?
* We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Albert Shoes gift cards.
How can I track my order?

* If your shoes are damaged upon arrival, please contact our customer service immediately for assistance.
